The Prophet Muhammad's ﷺ supplication for a newly-wed couple

It was narrated from Abu Hurairah: that the Prophet used to say, when offering congratulations of the occasion of marriage :

Abu Hurairah ؓ se Riwayat hai, unhone Farmaya : Nabi ﷺ Jab Shaadi ki Mubarakbaad dete to Yun Farmate :

" اللہ تمہیں برکت دے ، اور تم پر برکت نازل فرمائے ، اور تم دونوں کو خیر میں اکٹھا کرے ۔”

"Barak Allahu lakum, wa barak 'alaikum, wa jama'a bainakuma fi khair”

May Allah bless you and bestow blessings upon you, and bring you together in harmony.
"Allah Tumhe Barkat de, aur tum par barkat Nazil farmaye, aur tum Dono ko Khair me Ikattha kare."

Sunan Ibn Majah 1905
